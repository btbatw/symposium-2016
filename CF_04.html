Taiwan Liposome Company (TLC) 台灣微脂體

Taiwan Liposome Company (TLC) is a biopharmaceutical company focused on the research, development and commercialization of innovative pharmaceutical products based on its proprietary drug delivery technologies. Our strengths lie in lipid-based formulation and scale-up for parenteral drugs using micelles and nanoparticles to optimize the pharmacokinetics of drugs for better efficacy and lower toxicity, and thus prolong the product lifecycle of branded drugs.

TLC strives to become a global leading biopharmaceutical company, to contribute more towards making a difference in the healthcare industry and make Taiwan biotechnology industry visible in the global arena. Its headquarters is located in Taipei and subsidiaries in the US, the Netherlands, China, Australia, and Japan.
