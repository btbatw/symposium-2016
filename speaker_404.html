Tang-Long Shen, Ph.D. 沈湯龍 博士

Associate Professor, Department of Plant Pathology and Microbiology, NTU

Tang-Long Shen received his PhD in Molecular Medicine from Cornell University in 2003. He then went to American Heart Association for one year post-doctoral training before he joined Department of Plant Pathology and Microbiology at National Taiwan University, as an Assistant Professor, in 2004. Dr. Shen’s group is interested in the signal transduction pathways, especially in integrin and growth factor signaling.
