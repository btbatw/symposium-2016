Fountain Biopharma Inc.

PFountain was founded in December 2010 and headquartered in the Nankang Software Park, Taipei, Taiwan. Fountain Biopharma focused on innovative development of humanized & fully human monoclonal antibodies for the treatment of allergic disorders and cancers. The company has instituted needed core technologies for antibody drug discovery and development including fully human antibody phage display, screening bioanalytics, and antibody-guided delivery through collaboration with several leading academic institutes.
